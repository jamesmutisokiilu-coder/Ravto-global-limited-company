About Us

RAVTO GLOBAL LTD is a technical services provider based in Kenya, specializing in the design, installation, and maintenance of electrical systems, electronic security, and renewable energy solutions. The company serves residential, commercial, and agricultural sectors, focusing on energy efficiency and modern infrastructure.

Our Goal: “To provide reliable, affordable, and innovative solar, electrical, and security solutions that improve energy efficiency, enhance safety, and promote sustainable development across Kenya and beyond.”
